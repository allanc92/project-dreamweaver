Your Story · 148 words
Once upon a time, on a beach kissed by moonlight, there lived a small koala named Milo. Every night he walked the shore, collecting pebbles and sea-glass that caught the silver glow.

One evening, a beautiful shell tumbled through the waves — but it wasn't just any shell. It shimmered with warmth, and Milo knew at once it was lost.

"I'll find its family," he said softly.

He padded along the shore, past a surprised crab who offered directions, and over slippery rocks that gleamed like mirrors. When the tide rushed in, Milo hugged a friendly driftwood log and floated safely across.

At last he found a little shell-house tucked in the dunes. A tiny sea-snail family gasped with joy. "Our treasure!" they cheered.

Milo smiled all the way home, the stars winking above him like old friends.
